About the application

This is an Alternative Speech application initialy destinated to children with speaking disabilities, but could also be (partially) used as language learning tool. Go to the usage manual

The application started as a tiny prototype during a short semester project at the Laboratory of audio-visual communications of EPFL (Swiss Federal Institute of Technology). Afterwards it continued to be developed by V.Zemleris during his free time as is distributed for free. Donate to let the further improvements happen faster.

Thanks to David Romero, José Manuel Marcos and to many other people for the testing, ideas etc.

Usage manual

Icons and color coding. As in many other communicators, the icons have different background depending on their function in the language (more info):

  • Proper names: yellow
  • Common names: orange
  • Actions: green
  • Descriptives (adjectives, adverbs,…): blue
  • Social: pink
  • Miscellanea: white

    • Basic usage, history. Enter a phrase by clicking on icons and then to speak it press button .
    After ‘speaking’ you will be able to find the last phrases in the Menu->History. Also you’ll see the most used words on top of each category in bold.

    Automatic inflection, tenses, my gender, etc: One can select tenses on the top (past simple, futur simple, and present by default).  Simple French/English grammar is applied automatically, so you don’t have to inflect the words etc.

    Automatic form selection for verbs The app will choose between gerund/infinitive for modals to like doig smf, to want to do smf (more words will be added soon)

    for example:
    I want celebrate "The Three Kings" --> I want to celebrate The Three Kings.
    I like eat chocolade --> I like eating chocolade

    Searching for words: (Menu->Find word OR [Search] button if your devices has it):

    1. then no category selected, it will include all 5000 words (even more specific ones that are not yet tagged)
    2. then category selected, it will search only within that  category
    3. if you gesture search is available one could search by drawing letters
    1. otherwise you could use an onscreen keyboard [for tablets]

    Deleting words. Short-click delete button to delete one last icon. To delete the entire phrase long-click the button.

    Specific to smaller devices (mobile phones)

    To save space, the home screen has been divided into two parts. First contains the most used words, while sencond has icons for more specific categories.

    To switch between the the second screen slide/swype your finger to the right through the whole screen; swype left to return

    More Complex phrases:

    you may specify multiple nouns for subject, or object, add qualities for them. E.g:

    Input: Mother grand-father to-eat cereals milk

    Output: Mother and father eat cereals and milk

    Automatic Gender selection (applicable to French)

    For instance if you set your gender to feminin (Menu->Preferences), the gender will be automatically applied:

    je etre negation gros → je suis pas grosse

    The gender is also automatically applied for adjectives describing nouns that have gender:

    je suis pas un* gros garcon (this will work even if your gender if feminin)

    *note: selection of right determinants still have to implemented in the user interface
